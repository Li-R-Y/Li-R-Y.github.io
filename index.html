Hi! I am Ruoyu Li, an advocate for the dark sky movement and responsible outdoor lighting, have experience working in startups and NGOs, and am currently a graduate student in Urban Science and Informatics at NYU’s Center for Urban Science and Progress (CUSP).

I graduated from the University of Arizona with a B.S. in Mathematics (emphasizing Economics and Business), a B.S. in Information Science and Technology, minors in Statistics and Data Science, Computer Science, and Finance, and a certificate in Entrepreneurship and New Venture Development. I also received a Graduate Certificate in Advanced Business from Columbia University and the Credential of Readiness (CORe) from Harvard Business School Online.

I am passionate about using data to drive urban sustainability and improve community engagement.
